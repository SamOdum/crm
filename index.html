Welcome to Cross River Market

An initiative of Governor Ben Ayade, the Digital Governor of Cross River State of Nigeria, powered by the Cross River State Department of e-Commerce.

With the increasing effect of the Covid-19 pandemic, and in line with the Governor Ben Ayade's drive and passion to keep the people of Cross River State safe, the Governor has setup Cross River Market, an online-eCommerce portal to reduce the level of human physical interaction in the state, to prevent the spread of Covid-19, but increase the level of trade and e-Commerce in the State

This portal is the first of its kind by a sub-national government, and will ensure the development, protection, and increse in commercial activities in Cross River State and beyond.

Bong Duke Special Adviser, Government of Cross River State
